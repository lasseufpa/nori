37 /**
38  * \ingroup utils
39  *
40  * This class is an ns-3 trace sink that performs the calculation of
41  * RLC and PDCP statistics for uplink and downlink.
42  *
43  * Statistics are generated on a per radio bearer basis.
44  *
45  * The statistics are calculated at consecutive time windows and
46  * periodically written to a file. The calculated statistics are:
47  *
48  * - Number of transmitted PDUs
49  * - Number of received PDUs
50  * - Number of transmitted bytes
51  * - Number of received bytes
52  * - Average, min, max and standard deviation of PDU delay (delay is
53  * calculated from the generation of the PDU to its reception)
54  * - Average, min, max and standard deviation of PDU size
55  */
56
